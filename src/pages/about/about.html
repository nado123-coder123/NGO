Who We Are

About Shajar

Shajar Hope Alliance is a modern non-profit initiative focused on empowering extraordinary individuals through education and financial support. We help people grow, just like a tree, by providing opportunities that build stronger futures.

🌱

Our Mission

To provide interest-free financial assistance and secure scholarships to deserving individuals so they can achieve stability, excellent education, and long-term professional success.

🌍

Our Vision

A connected world where financial hardship never prevents talented and driven individuals from achieving their wildest dreams and contributing positively to our global society.

Our Financial Transparency Policy

All infrastructure, server, and administrative operational costs are privately funded by our founding members. We strictly do not take a percentage out of general donations.

We never transfer money to student's personal accounts. All scholarship funds are wired directly to the educational institution's official registrar or finance office.
